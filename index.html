The Science

Predictive Scale-Down.
Industrial Fidelity.

Traditional bioreactor development forces companies into costly pilot cycles with unpredictable outcomes. Nexxar's M-STaR platform changes the equation entirely.

Scale-Down Simulation

Replicate industrial tank conditions — mixing, oxygen transfer, gradients — inside a compact lab-scale vessel. What you see at 1L, you get at 100,000L.

Real-Time Sensing

Continuous monitoring of dissolved oxygen, pH, temperature, and metabolite profiles. High-cell-density fermentation data captured at millisecond resolution.

Compressed Timeline

Parallel screening and iterative optimization in weeks, not years. The Upscale platform reduces scale-up decision timelines by up to 70%.

Modular Architecture

Configurable vessel geometries and mixing modules. Adapt the Upscale system to any fermentation process — bacteria, yeast, algae, or mammalian cell culture.

The Upscale System

One Platform.
Every Fermentation Process.

The Upscale is Nexxar's flagship scale-down bioreactor system — a compact, precision instrument that faithfully mimics the hydrodynamic and mass transfer conditions of industrial-scale tanks.

Built on 15+ years of DTU Biosustain fermentation research, Upscale eliminates the guesswork that has historically made biomanufacturing scale-up one of the costliest steps in biotech development.

About Nexxar

From DTU Research
to Global Market

Nexxar ApS is a Danish biotechnology innovation company incorporated in December 2025, built on two decades of fermentation research at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU Biosustain).

Our mission is to accelerate biosolutions implementation — giving every biotech innovator the tools to predict industrial bioprocess behavior with confidence and compress the path from molecule to manufacture.

Headquartered in Søborg, Denmark, Nexxar is backed by pre-seed funding and actively seeking Series A/Seed partners to scale globally through 2026–2027.
